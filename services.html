Our Services

At O-Town Bike Repair, we are dedicated to keeping you and your bike on the road and trail with expert service and care. No matter if it's repairs, rentals or even upgrades, the experts at O-Town Bike Repair. With a focus on quality and personalized attention, we strive to exceed your expectations and ensure your biking experience is always smooth and enjoyable.

Rentals

Explore the beauty of Ottawa with our convenient bike rental service. Whether you're a visitor looking to explore the area or a local in need of temporary transportation, we have a diverse selection of bikes available for rent to suit your needs.

Our rentals include:

  • Mountain Bikes
  • Road Bikes
  • Hybrid Bikes
  • Kids' Bikes
  • Electric Bikes

Our rental rates are affordable and flexible, with options for hourly, daily, and weekly rentals. All rentals include a helmet and lock for your safety and security. Discounts may be available for extended rental periods or group bookings.

Repairs

Our repair services are designed to address any issues your bike may encounter, from minor adjustments to major fixes. Our experienced technicians will diagnose the problem and provide expert solutions to get you back on the road in no time.

Our services include:

  • Flat Tire Repair
  • Brake Adjustment and Replacement
  • Gear Adjustment and Replacement
  • Pedal Replacement
  • Hub Overhaul
  • Frame Welding and Repair
  • Handlebar and Stem Adjustment
  • Seat and Seatpost Replacement
