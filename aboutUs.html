Who Are We?


Welcome to O-Town Bike Repair, Ottawa's best destination for all your bicycle repair and servicing needs. We are a locally-owned and operated shop dedicated to keeping Ottawa's cycling community on the move.

Founded in 1997, our team of skilled and passionate technicians bring years of experience and a deep love for cycling to every repair. From minor adjustments to major overhauls, we have the skills and the tools to get the job done. Whether you are a casual biker or a professional of any variety, we understand the unique demands of Ottawa's diverse cycling landscape.

In addition to repairs, we offer a selection of high-quality parts and accessories to enhance your riding experience. Our friendly staff is always ready to offer expert advice and recommendations to help you make the most of your cycling adventures.

Join the many satisfied customers who have made O-Town Bike Repair their go-to bike repair shop in Ottawa. Ride with confidence, knowing your bike is in the hands of professionals who care as much about your ride as you do.

Come visit us today and experience the O-Town Bike Repair difference. Let's keep Ottawa riding!
